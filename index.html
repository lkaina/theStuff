I'm a software engineer in the SF Bay Area that enjoys dabbling in different technologies. While most of my experience has been with front-end web apps, I've also explored mobile development in both native iOS and PhoneGap.

Before making the leap into software development I spent 7 years doing econometric analyses in an academcially focused environment in Cambridge, MA. I made the transition into software in order to develop a skill-set that will help me build out my own ideas, and also to discover more about the field of data science and machine learning.

I created Donator(working title) with the sole purpose of simplifying the process of making charitable contributions. In the current working version, in-app donation is not yet functional but the idea would be to eventually give users the ability to make minimal contributions to organizations they feel best represent the user's own interest.
