PhoneTag is the product of a 4-man team working over a 2 week period. The game mechanics are similar to those of paintball/laser-tag, with your smartphone being the only equipment required. Track your enemies in real-time using your phone to navigate the battlefield. Run to pick up power-ups that will give you a leg up on your enemies, but be sure to avoid a few surprises along the way. PhoneTag brings digital gaming back from off of the couch and into the real world.
